Event Details

The Republic Day Celebration 2024, scheduled for January 26 at the National Parade Ground in chandigarh, promises a day filled with patriotic fervor and excitement. Starting with the dignified flag hoisting ceremony by Chief Guest VIVEKANAND, the event features a dazzling parade showcasing the country's diversity. The day unfolds with the President's address, followed by a thrilling missile show displaying defense capabilities. Cultural performances by renowned artists highlight the nation's heritage. An adrenaline-pumping air show, featuring fighter jets and aerobatic displays, captivates the audience. The festivities culminate with a breathtaking fireworks display, creating a memorable and patriotic atmosphere for all.
